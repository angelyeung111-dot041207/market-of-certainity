# Market of Certainty: How Prediction Platforms Commodify Democracy

## Project Overview
This project investigates the ethical implications and power dynamics inherent in modern prediction platforms such as **Kalshi** and **Polymarket**. Our research focuses on how these markets often facilitate information inequality, allowing well-resourced speculators to profit from social and political narratives at the expense of marginalized groups.

## Core Research Themes
* **Data Justice:** Analyzing the information asymmetry between retail traders and elite speculators.
* **Commodification of Democracy:** Examining how civic decisions and geopolitical crises are transformed into tradable assets.
* **Exclusionary Structures:** Highlighting how those most affected by market outcomes (e.g., political unrest) are often excluded from the "wisdom of the crowd."
